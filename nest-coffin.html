Nest Coffin

◼ A coffin designed for minimal environmental impact
◼ 2022
◼ 4 months
◼ With Clemence Nollim

Challenge

The task was to develop a coffin for the eco-conscious consumer in the French market. This required the use of biodegradable materials that comply with the relatively strict French regulations concerning impermeability and structural integrity. The typical coffin, made out of wood or wood-substitutes, contains resins and lacquers that are toxic to bury or burn, so the partner asked that we develop aesthetically pleasing alternatives.
