I’m a user experience and product designer based in Paris.

from philosophy

During my undergraduate education, I had the opportunity to develop a solid foundation of research and critical thinking skills while working towards my bachelor’s in political science and philosophy. These skills were bolstered by my professional experience as a researcher that I acquired during and after my undergraduate studies.

to art

I was fortunate to be exposed to the arts from an early age, and after acquiring my diploma, I found myself longing to work with my hands again. I interned at two art studios while working as an assistant for a professional artist. Eventually, I received the opportunity to travel to China for a master’s of fine arts on a full scholarship from the Chinese government. There, I was able to further develop my aesthetic and conceptual sensibilities while immersing myself in the local culture.

to design

During my master’s, my interests began to gravitate towards design. Design, as a concept, seemed like the perfect mix of my interests and skills. I understand design to be a combination of research, critical thinking, and aesthetics, with the added challenge of empathy. I believe that good design requires empathizing with others to solve problems, fulfill needs, and hopefully improve lives. It’s a challenge I find both exhilarating and fulfilling. I’m currently pursuing a master’s in industrial design.
